Break the habit, Quit E-cigarette

-A program inspired by NOT (Not-on-Tobacco) that targets the youth seeking help in quitting smoking. This project will consist of 8 sessions with various activities centered on providing support in maintaining self-control throughout their course in this program.


The 8 Sessions

Session 1: All about vaping and its chemicals

Session 2: Causes of Vaping

Session 3: Effects of Vaping

Session 4: Breath Free - A NOVA Talk: Journey in Quitting Vaping

Session 5: What are my reasons for vaping?

Session 6: Why do I want to quit vaping?

Session 7: Stress Management: Healthy Coping Mechanisms

Session 8: Mural Oath: NOVA Wall
